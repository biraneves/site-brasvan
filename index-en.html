Our company

We are a travel agency specialized in receptive tourism and focused on the tourist trade of Sao Paulo.

Directed by professionals with experience acquired in various areas, and working with highly qualified people, we follow each stage of the project to be carried out, ensuring the total success of your event.

Our service

We are located just 10 minutes from Guarulhos International Airport and offer, for your convenience:

  • 24/7 service;
  • receptionists specialized in passenger assistance;
  • complimentary bilingual English and Spanish service (other languages: ask us).

Events

For the realization of events, we always select qualified, experienced and regulated professionals for the development of the various activities, according to the requirements demanded by the contractor for the performance of the designated function.

We work in partnership with experienced and respected companies in the market to rent the necessary equipment, such as audio, video, lighting, computers and others. Thus, we can ensure better suitability and greater efficiency in using the requested material.

Receptive

We arrange arrival and departure transfers according to the provided domestic or international air program in order to suggest the most suitable vehicle for the transportation of passengers, with the shortest waiting time at airports.

All reception, accompaniment, accommodation of passengers in vehicles and check-in at the airport or hotel are always under the guidance of receptionists, trained to provide complete assistance to visitors.

We offer, as needed, the most varied types of vehicles, such as executive cars (armored or not), vans, minibuses, buses and helicopters, intended for the various phases of the event, thus ensuring greater organization and operation of logistics.

City Tours

We work with well-trained guides to take you to the most interesting spots in the city. With half day and full day programs, you can visit parks, squares, shopping malls, restaurants, bars, museums, theaters, stadiums, churches and historic sites, with full explanations in your own language.

Accommodation

We maintain a record of hotels with different categories of services and comfort, at the best rates, to adequately meet the needs of your stay.

Vehicles

Our transfers, trips and tours are carried out in high standard executive vehicles, vans, minibuses and buses, all with air conditioning, to provide the utmost comfort and safety to our users.

For your event, we also have vehicles sedan, SUV, minivan, armored and imported with all necessary ans essential options to meet the requirements of each customer.
